# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful
- Being constructive
- Helping others learn
- Accepting feedback

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment
- Hate speech
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination

Let's build Frijolito OS together in a friendly and collaborative environment.
